Admission Terms & Conditions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age limit: Minimum 17 years and Maximum 30 years.
  • Minimum qualification 10+2 with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) with at least 45% marks.
  • Admission is conducted once every academic year.
  • Candidate must be medically fit.
  • Admission will follow Indian Nursing Council & UP State Medical Faculty rules.

Admission Selection Committee

  • Chairman / Principal
  • Vice Principal
  • Senior Tutor
  • Chief Nursing Officer / Nursing Superintendent
  • Administrative Staff

Training Period

Diploma in General Nursing & Midwifery duration is 3 Years.

A preliminary training period of 12 weeks including ward observation and supervised practice (not exceeding 16 hours duty).

Vacation & Holidays

  • Annual Vacation – 30 Days
  • Sick Leave – 10 Days per year
  • Preparatory Leave – 7 Days per year
  • Sick leave cannot be accumulated year to year.
